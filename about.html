Meet the Founder

🎀 Founder & Creative Director

Hello, I'm Margaret 🌸

the girl behind Garetquettes 🎀

Garetquettes is a soft, delicate little world inspired by the feeling of being a porcelain doll — gentle, timeless, and effortlessly beautiful. It’s a space made for girls who adore all things coquette: dainty details, soft silhouettes, ribbons, lace, and that quiet kind of elegance that feels both fragile and powerful at the same time ✧

Every piece you’ll find here is carefully chosen to capture that dreamy, doll-like charm — the kind that makes you feel pretty in the simplest moments, like you’re living inside your own romantic daydream. Whether it’s a sweet outfit, a charming accessory, or a tiny detail that completes your look, Garetquettes is here to make you feel soft, feminine, and a little bit magical 💌

Because sometimes, the most beautiful thing you can be is soft — like a porcelain doll, delicate yet unforgettable. So come step into my world, where everything is a little softer, sweeter, and made just for you ♡
